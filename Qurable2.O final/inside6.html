Stress is a normal human reaction that happens to everyone. In fact, the human body is designed to experience stress and react to it. When you experience changes or challenges (stressors), your body produces physical and mental responses. Thatâ€™s stress. Stress responses help your body adjust to new situations. But stress becomes a problem when stressors continue without relief or periods of relaxation.
